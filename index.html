Welcome to Casa Victoria Hotel

Experience unparalleled scenery and family-oriented travel experiences at one of Mexico's most popular tourist destinations, Rocky Point. Casa Victoria is a hotel located in the heart of Rocky Point, Mexico, just over a mile from the ocean. This family-owned and operated hotel is perfect for travelers looking to experience the beauty Mexico has to offer on a budget. The hotel exudes a warm, homey vibe that welcomes everyone. Conveniently situated near popular tourist spots like El Malecón, Casa Victoria offers easy access to the best attractions. We would be honored to have you stay with us and be part of your beautiful experience.
